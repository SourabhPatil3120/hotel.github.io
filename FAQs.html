Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I book a room?

To book a room, simply browse our available hotels, choose your preferred location and dates, and follow the easy steps to complete your booking. You can also sign up for an account to save your details for future bookings.

2. Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 24 hours before your check-in time. Cancellation policies vary by hotel, so be sure to check the specific hotel’s policy when making a reservation.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We Currently unable to offer PayPal and bank transfer options for the hotel bookings.

4. Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Many of our hotels offer breakfast as part of the room rate, but this varies by hotel. Please check the specific hotel's details before making a booking.

5. Can I request extra amenities (e.g., baby crib, late check-out)?

Yes, most hotels offer additional amenities upon request, such as cribs, late check-out, and more. Please make the request when booking, or contact the hotel directly to confirm availability.

6. Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

We offer special promotions from time to time, such as early bird discounts or last-minute deals. Be sure to subscribe to our newsletter or check our website regularly to stay updated on the latest offers.

7. What should I do if I have a problem during my stay?

If you experience any issues during your stay, please contact the hotel’s front desk immediately. If you need further assistance, you can reach out to our customer service team for support.

8. Is it safe to book through your website?

Yes, we take your security and privacy seriously. Our website uses encryption technology (SSL) to ensure that all your personal information and payment details are securely transmitted.

9. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by emailing us at support@hotelbooking.com or calling our toll-free number at 1-800-123-4567. We’re available 24/7 to assist you.

10. Can I book a hotel for someone else?

Yes, you can book a hotel room for someone else. During the booking process, simply provide the guest's details in the required fields.
